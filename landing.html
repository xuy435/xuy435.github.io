About Puff Lab

Puff Anatomy is an interactive exploration of pufferfish anatomy presented through non-linear text.
Instead of reading information in a fixed order, users click, discover, and experience each organ individually. Text appears dynamically, falling into the screen like drifting objects in water.
This project transforms biological information into a playful interface. It invites curiosity rather than instruction, suggesting that learning can be exploratory, intuitive, and slightly unpredictable â€” just like the ocean itself.
